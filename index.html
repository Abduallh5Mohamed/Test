Ai-Exams

AI-Exams is an advanced digital platform established by eight students from the Faculty of Artificial Intelligence at Delta University for Science and Technology. Officially launched in 2025, AI-Exams specializes in providing smart and secure solutions for online assessments. The platform leverages cutting-edge artificial intelligence technologies to offer highly efficient cheating detection, ensuring integrity in digital examinations. It features a simple and user-friendly interface, enabling instructors to effortlessly add and manage exam questions. AI-Exams prioritizes absolute security, safeguarding exam content and user data through robust protection protocols. Established through innovative collaboration by eight students from the Faculty of Artificial Intelligence at Delta University, AI-Exams represents a significant advancement towards smart and secure online examination practices.
